General website

Blogs

The ability to create and edit blog posts with text formatting, link creation, easy image or video insertion, and more with no HTML skills required helps make blogging a breeze.

Ecommerce

A CMS allows non-technical users to change the look and feel of the online store, add and remove products or services, change page layouts, promote certain products, and much more.
